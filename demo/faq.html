FAQ

Q: Why don't use Hadoop?
A: Hadoop (as great as it is) relies on the fact that all data that is to be processed is collected and stored in one place. It's just then that Hadoop can do real processing. This is completely different from what we have with the Streaming Analytics platform where we have to deal with streamed data. This means that the Streaming Analytics platform needs to process data while it is being streamed and do it near real-time.
